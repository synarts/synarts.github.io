Kauer

Kauer is a full commission suit that is made for a customer, he’s a plantigrade rat suit.

Features

  • Two replaceable USB powered blowers
  • All seams on the outside of the face are machine sewn, the ears are sewn onto the face
  • Hidden AirTag pocket
  • Base made from soft expanding polyurethane foam
  • Removable / replaceable 3D eyes
  • Soft rubbery ears
  • Socketed replaceable teeth
  • Size adjustable liner
  • Fully castinplace lined interior
  • Shaped neck
  • Velcro-in-place tongue
  • Unique QR code for care instructions and details on the fabrics used to make him.

Construction

The head is made from cast polyurethane foam with 3D printed eyes and 3D printed teeth and is fully lined, with two high speed blowers. The head is cast in foam so that it soft and comfrotable to wear in a variety of situations.
