Natural Color Spotted Hyena

Is a mini-partial suit made to sell at DenFur 2024

Features

  • Two replaceable USB powered blowers

  • All seams on the outside of the face are machine sewn, the ears are sewn onto the face

  • Hidden AirTag pocket

  • Base made from soft expanding polyurethane foam

  • Removable / replaceable 3D eyes (includes additional set of eye backers)

  • Removable / interchangable eye-lids

  • Rubbery ears

  • Socketed teeth

  • Size adjustable liner

  • Fully cast-in-place lined interior

  • Shaped neck

  • Unique QR code for care instructions and details on the fabrics

  • Tail with zipper to remove stuffing

  • Custom ‘haha haha’ embroidery on the tail

  • Flaws

Notable repair on the bridge of the nose between the eyes

There was a bald spot in the fur that wasn’t caught until final finishing and a repair has been performed, the bald spot was removed and a new patch of matching fur was glued in place. The owner of this suit should avoid brushing this area to insure that the repair remains in place.

Construction

The head is made from cast polyurethane foam with 3D printed eyes and 3D printed teeth and is fully lined, with two high speed blowers. The head is cast in foam so that it soft and comfortable to wear in a variety of situations.

If ordering additional materials for this head, please order samples of the fabrics listed below to verify the dye lots and products match and that the correct materials have been listed below.
