Rusty

Rusty is a full plantigrade wild boar fursuit I made for myself, he was made on a whim. I choose natural colors and I really wanted an aggressive punky look.

Construction

The head is made from cast polyurethane foam with 3D printed eyes and is fully lined, with two high speed blowers.
