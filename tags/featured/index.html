Albino Rat

Is a mini-partial suit made to sell at DenFur 2024

Features

  • Two replaceable USB powered blowers
  • All seams on the outside of the face are machine sewn, the ears are sewn onto the face
  • Hidden AirTag pocket
  • Base made from soft expanding polyurethane foam
  • Removable / replaceable 3D eyes (includes additional set of eyes)
  • Soft rubbery ears
  • Socketed replaceable teeth
  • Size adjustable liner
  • Fully castinplace lined interior
  • Shaped neck
  • Unique QR code for care instructions and details on the fabrics used to make him
  • Floor dragger tail with zipper and pocket containing beads so it can easily be washed
  • Custom heart shape embroidery on the tail
  • Loop on tail to allow it to be attached to a belt so it doesn’t drag on the floor
  • NFC tag on left size of muzzle

Construction

The head is made from cast polyurethane foam with 3D printed eyes and 3D printed teeth and is fully lined, with two high speed blowers. The head is cast in foam so that it soft and comfortable to wear in a variety of situations.

If ordering additional materials for this head, please order samples of the fabrics listed below to verify the dye lots and products match.

Materials used:

  • Base & Liner
    • Polyurethane expanding foam
    • BigZFabric Microfiber Headliner Pink
    • BigZFabric Microfiber Black
    • BigZFabric Costume Spandex
    • Joann Headliner Fabric
  • Ears
    • 3D printed TPU ears
    • Howl Fabrics Cream Luxury Teddy
  • Eyes
    • Dye sublimated PVC Mesh for eyes, custom ordered mesh
    • 3D printed ABS Printed sockets Polymaker ABS Pink
  • Teeth
    • 3D printed TPU teeth
  • Face mask
    • Howl Fabrics Cream Luxury Teddy
    • BigZFabric Microfiber Black (Lips)
    • BibZFabric Stretch Mochi Blush (Nose)
